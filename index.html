who are we

"Greetings, we are Pet adoption center, a non-profit organization located in the heart of St. Louis. Our core mission centers on addressing the challenges associated with pet overpopulation and the resulting hardships within our community. We are wholly committed to the rescue, rehabilitation, and successful placement of cats, dogs, kittens, and puppies who have found themselves in unfortunate circumstances."
